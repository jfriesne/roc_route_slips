This ride accompanies the A Ride and breaks off early to avoid the Harbor Blvd climb. We all meet at the same coffee shop. The B riders can wait for the A riders and then we all ride back together. The ride is 37 miles with 920 feet of climbing.

Please download the route slip: https://raw.githubusercontent.com/jfriesne/roc_route_slips/master/B_Ride_Parnell_Park_Harbor_Blvd.txt

The route map can be found at: http://www.mapmyride.com/routes/view/1964527805
