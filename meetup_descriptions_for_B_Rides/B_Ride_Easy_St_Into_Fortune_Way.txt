This ride is the B ride associated with "Easy Street Into Fortune Way A Route". It's 36 miles long and avoids a lot of the climbing. Coffee stop at Panera Bakery.

Please download the route slip from this link (https://raw.githubusercontent.com/jfriesne/roc_route_slips/master/B_Ride_Easy_St_Into_Fortune_Way.txt), print it out, and keep it with you during the ride.
