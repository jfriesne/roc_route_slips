This ride is a ride up highway 39 past East Fork, and then up a paved service road to Cogswell dam.  The road to Cogswell dam is mild and pleasant except for the last mile or so which is quite steep (15%); those who don't like climbing can turn around a little early to avoid it.

The route slip can be downloaded from here:  https://raw.githubusercontent.com/jfriesne/roc_route_slips/master/A_Ride_Cogswell_Dam.txt
