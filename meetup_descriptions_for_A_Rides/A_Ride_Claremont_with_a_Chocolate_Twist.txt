This ride is the "Claremont with a Chocolate Twist" ride.  It is a 47 mile ride with 1,790 feet of elevation gain, and a stop at the Some Crust Bakery in Claremont.

Please download the route slip from this link (https://raw.githubusercontent.com/jfriesne/roc_route_slips/master/A_Ride_Claremont_with_a_Chocolate_Twist.txt), print it out, and keep it with you during the ride.

You can view a map of this route by clicking here (https://www.mapmyride.com/routes/view/6540812335/).
