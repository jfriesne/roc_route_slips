This ride is the "Claremont with a Twist" ride. It is a 45 mile ride with 1,300 feet of elevation gain, and a stop at the Euro Cafe in Claremont.

Please download the route slip from this link (https://raw.githubusercontent.com/jfriesne/roc_route_slips/master/A_Ride_Claremont_with_a_Twist.txt), print it out, and keep it with you during the ride.

You can view a map of this route by clicking here (http://veloroutes.org/r/92110).
