This is the Brea Canyon ride. It is a 52.5 mile ride with 1300 feet of elevation gain. The ride takes us by the 57 freeway, through the Canyon and then coffee on Grand.

Please download the route slip from this link (https://raw.githubusercontent.com/jfriesne/roc_route_slips/master/A_Ride_Brea_Canyon.txt), print it out, and keep it with you during the ride.
