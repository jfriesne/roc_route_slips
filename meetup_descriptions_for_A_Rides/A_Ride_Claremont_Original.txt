This is the CLAREMONT ORIGINAL RIDE which is a ride that takes us to the Coffee Bean and patio in downtown Claremont. The ride is 43 miles long and has an elevation gain of just over 1,200 ft. with gradual and moderate climbing.

Please download the route slip from this link (https://raw.githubusercontent.com/jfriesne/roc_route_slips/master/A_Ride_Claremont_Original.txt), print it out, and keep it with you during the ride.

You can view a map of this route by clicking here (http://www.mapmyride.com/routes/view/950340841).
