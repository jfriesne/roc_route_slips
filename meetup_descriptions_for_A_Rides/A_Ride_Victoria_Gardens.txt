This ride takes us to Victoria Gardens out by Rancho Cucamonga. It is a long ride, 64 miles with 2006 feet of elevation. To cut down on mileage people can meet at the Enchanto bridge. This reduces the ride to 54 miles. B rides can drop off at Claremont.
