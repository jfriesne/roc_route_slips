This is the Molly Souper ride to Molly's Souper in Upland for lunch.  It's a 52 mile ride, with 1,540 feet of elevation gain and lots of twists and turns.  We shamelessly stole this ride from the Foothill club.

The route map can be found at:  https://www.mapmyride.com/routes/view/5310431557

Please download the route slip from this link (https://raw.githubusercontent.com/jfriesne/roc_route_slips/master/A_Ride_Molly_Souper.txt), print it out, and keep it with you during the ride.
