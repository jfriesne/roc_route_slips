This ride which takes us West into Glendale via a convoluted route. For the A riders it is 53 miles and 2700 feet of climbing. For the B riders, 39 miles and 2070 feet of climbing. B riders start at Victory Park on Paloma. We have done this ride once before and enjoyed it.
