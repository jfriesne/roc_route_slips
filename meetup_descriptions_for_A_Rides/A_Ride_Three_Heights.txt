This ride is the Three Heights ride -- a popular 52 mile ride. Elevation gain is 1850 ft. Please download the route slip from this link (https://raw.githubusercontent.com/jfriesne/roc_route_slips/master/A_Ride_Three_Heights.txt), print it out, and keep it with you during the ride. You can view a map of this route by clicking here (http://veloroutes.org/r/92469).
