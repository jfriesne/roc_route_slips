This week's SATURDAY RIDE is the SAN DIMAS - BONELLI PARK RIDE. It is 40.3 miles long and has 1,350 ft. of elevation gain overall and has only moderate climbing in a couple areas.

Please download the route slip from this link (https://raw.githubusercontent.com/jfriesne/roc_route_slips/master/A_Ride_San_Dimas_Bonelli_Park.txt), print it out, and keep it with you during the ride.

You can view a map of this route by clicking here (http://veloroutes.org/r/92923).
