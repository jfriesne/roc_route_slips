Take a leisurely ride down the San Gabriel bike path to Rosecrans. Exit the bike path, grab coffee at Starbucks and then head home north on the Rio Hondo bike path.

B riders can start at Peck Park for a distance of about 40 miles. Give the A riders 20 to 25 minutes to get to Peck Park.
