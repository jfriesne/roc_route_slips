This ride takes us down the San Gabriel river trail to Seal Beach where coffee and goodies are available. Then off to Longbeach and up the Rio Hondo home. Route map is: https://raw.githubusercontent.com/jfriesne/roc_route_slips/master/A_Ride_Rio_Hondo_San_Gabriel_Lario_Rio_Hondo_Loop.txt

Rides have the option of starting at the following locations: Von's, Peck Park or Pico Riveria Golf course. Allow about 20 or 40 minutes for the group to reach you if you start from Peck Park or Pico golf course respectively. Please add in a comment if you are starting at any point other than Von's.
