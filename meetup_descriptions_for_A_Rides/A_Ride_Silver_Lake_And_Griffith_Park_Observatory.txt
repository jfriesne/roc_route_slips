The Saturday A ride is the SILVER LAKE AND GRIFFITH PARK OBSERVATORY RIDE. It is a ride of 54.5 miles and has about 1,900 ft. of elevation gain overall with a climb over Mt. Hollywood Dr. in Griffith Park.

Please download the route slip from this link (https://raw.githubusercontent.com/jfriesne/roc_route_slips/master/A_Ride_Silver_Lake_And_Griffith_Park_Observatory.txt), print it out, and keep it with you during the ride.
