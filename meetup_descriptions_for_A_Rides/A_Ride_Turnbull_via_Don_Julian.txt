This ride is the Turnbull Canyon Clockwise ride, a 42 mile ride with 1918 ft of elevation. Please download the route slip from this link ( https://raw.githubusercontent.com/jfriesne/roc_route_slips/master/A_Ride_Turnbull_Clockwise.txt ), print it out, and keep it with you during the ride. You can view a map of this route by clicking here ( http://www.mapmyride.com/routes/fullscreen/187522410/ ).
