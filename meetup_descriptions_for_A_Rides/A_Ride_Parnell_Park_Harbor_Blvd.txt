This week's A ride the PARNELL PARK & HARBOR BLVD RIDE. This ride goes through some familiar areas and has some fun hills that will challenge us. The elevation gains on this route are mostly gradual or moderate inclines but there are a few short steeper climbs and one longer (1.5 mile) steeper climb.

Please download the route slip from this link (https://raw.githubusercontent.com/jfriesne/roc_route_slips/master/A_Ride_Parnell_Park_Harbor_Blvd.txt), print it out, and keep it with you during the ride.

You can view a map of this route by clicking here (http://www.mapmyride.com/routes/view/1142275462).
